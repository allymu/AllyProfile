Hello, I’m Ally.

I am currently creating and exploring in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Background

I’m a UX designer with a strong visual design background.

I studied Graphic Design and Media Arts in college. I spent many years designing products and packaging for retail stores. A few years ago I made the transition to UX Design.

I love what I do and I am always looking for opportunities to expand my skills. I am currently working on becoming more proficient in front end development and video editing.

Goals

I want to master the process of building web sites and increase my knowledge, skills and abilities in:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

I know that these skills will help me become a more well rounded UX designer who collaborates well with developers.

I also want to be able to design and build my UX portfolio without using a template site.
